Peter Gene Hernandez (born October 8, 1985), known professionally as Bruno Mars, is an American singer-songwriter and record producer. He is known for his stage presence, retro showmanship, and performing in a variety of musical styles, including pop, R&B, funk, soul, reggae, disco, and rock. Mars was accompanied by his band, the Hooligans, who played a variety of instruments, such as electric guitar, bass, piano, keyboards, drums, and trumpet, and also served as backup singers and dancers
